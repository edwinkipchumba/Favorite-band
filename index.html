biography

Jean de Dieu Makiese, popularly known as Madillu system, was a Congolese rhumba singer and songwriter. Born 1n 1952 and died in 2007. He was once a member of the seminal band TPOK Jazz which dominated the Congolese scene from 1960s through 1980s.Beginning as a teenager in 1969, Madilu sang with a series of bands: "Orchestre Symba", "Orchestre Bamboula", headed by Papa Noel, "Festival des Maquisards", led by Sam Mangwana, and Fiesta Popular.
